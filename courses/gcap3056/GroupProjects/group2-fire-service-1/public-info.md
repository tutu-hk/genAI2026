# Group 2: Fire Service 1

## Project Title
Fire Service Analysis - Learning from the Past

## Overview
This project focuses on analyzing Hong Kong fire service incidents, emergency response protocols, and lessons learned from past fire emergencies to improve public safety policies.

## Direction

The group is investigating:
- Historical fire incidents in Hong Kong
- Emergency notification systems and alert messages sent to residents during fires
- Methods to notify citizens during fire emergencies
- Lessons learned from past incidents

---

## Key Areas of Investigation

### 1. Alert Message Systems
- Review of how alert messages were sent to residents during past fires
- Effectiveness of current notification methods
- Comparison with international best practices

### 2. Emergency Response Protocols
- Fire service response times
- Coordination between departments
- Public communication during emergencies

### 3. Historical Case Studies
- Analysis of significant fire incidents
- Identification of systemic issues
- Policy changes implemented post-incident

---

## Research Framework

| Category | Focus Area | Questions |
|----------|------------|-----------|
| Notification | Alert Systems | How effectively were residents notified? |
| Response | Emergency Services | What was the response time and coordination? |
| Prevention | Building Safety | What preventive measures were in place? |
| Learning | Policy Changes | What improvements were made afterward? |
