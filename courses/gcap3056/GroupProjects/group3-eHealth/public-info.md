# Group 3: eHealth

## Project title
eHealth — automation of insurance reimbursement for medical bills (technical infrastructure)

## Overview
This project explores how **eHealth** in Hong Kong may support **automation of insurance reimbursement** for medical bills, with a focus on **technical infrastructure**. The team will also search for and use **SCMP letters on eHealth**, including some authored by Simon Wang (course teacher), to inform the analysis and questions for government.

## Focus areas
- eHealth policy and technical infrastructure in HK
- Insurance reimbursement processes and automation
- Public information and SCMP letters on eHealth (including by Simon Wang)
