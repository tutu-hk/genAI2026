## Project Topics

### Group 1: Flu Shot
**Seasonal influenza vaccination program and public health campaigns in Hong Kong.**

Focus on analyzing the government's flu vaccination promotion strategies, uptake rates, and data transparency regarding immunization coverage.

---

### Group 2: Colorectal Cancer Screening Programme
**Government-subsidized screening program for colorectal cancer prevention and early detection.**

Investigate the effectiveness of the screening program, public awareness campaigns, and data availability on screening outcomes.

---

### Group 3: Road Safety
**Road safety policies, traffic management, and accident prevention initiatives.**

Analyze traffic accident data, government safety measures, and the evidence base behind road safety policies in Hong Kong.

---

### Group 4: eMPF
**Electronic Mandatory Provident Fund platform for streamlined pension fund management.**

Examine the eMPF digital transformation, user adoption data, and transparency of pension fund governance.

---

### Group 5: Chronic Disease Co-Care (CDCC) Pilot Scheme
**Primary healthcare pilot scheme for chronic disease management through public-private partnership.**

Evaluate the pilot scheme's data collection, outcomes measurement, and policy implications for primary healthcare reform.

---

### Group 6: Rodent Control
**Urban pest management and public hygiene initiatives for rodent prevention across Hong Kong districts.**

Investigate rodent infestation data by district, government response measures, and the effectiveness of pest control policies.

---

### Group 7: Bus Stop Merge
**Real-Time API Data Analysis and Bus Stop Placement Optimization in Hong Kong.**

Analyze public transport data, bus stop utilization patterns, and use data to propose optimization strategies.

---

## Project Requirements

Each group will:

1. **Request government data** from relevant departments
2. **Conduct fieldwork** to collect primary empirical data
3. **Analyze data** using AI-powered tools
4. **Prepare deliverables:**
   - Group report (~3,000 words)
   - Poster
   - Two presentations
5. **Submit to LegCo** via the redress system as a complaint about government data governance
