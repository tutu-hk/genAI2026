## Core research question (Fall 2025)

**How can mathematical models and improved data governance enable more effective data-driven decision-making for typhoon signal timing and emergency response coordination?**

Focus areas: signal timing optimization, wind speed prediction, emergency response coordination, data governance and transparency.

---

## What the team did

### 1. Mathematical models for typhoon signal optimization

- **Regression analysis:** Signal timing accuracy (actual vs predicted wind); factors: announcement lead time, meteorological data quality, historical patterns.
- **Time series:** ARIMA for wind speed prediction; optimal announcement timing.
- **Simulation:** Monte Carlo for emergency response scenarios; population, evacuation routes, shelter capacity.
- **Optimization:** Minimize economic disruption while maximizing public safety; variables: announcement timing, wind thresholds, response coordination.

### 2. Data governance assessment

- **Current practices:** Meteorological data collection; signal decision documentation; emergency response measurement.
- **Gaps:** Limited data on signal timing accuracy; economic impact; public response; emergency coordination.
- **Improved framework:** Systematic data collection; real-time monitoring; stakeholder access; accountability metrics.
