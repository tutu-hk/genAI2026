**Week 3 | Individual**

Regression analysis using Jupyter Notebook with Hong Kong Waste Charging Policy survey data.

### In-Class Exercise 2 (5%)
**Week 4 | Individual**

Simulation modeling exercise using SimPy for bus transit analysis.

## Reflective Essays (20%)

### Reflective Essay 1
**Week 5 | Individual | Part of 20%**

Reflect on regression & simulation models and their connection to your group project.

### Reflective Essay 2
**Week 7 | Individual | Part of 20%**

### Reflective Essay 3
**Week 9 | Individual | Part of 20%**

## Presentations (25%)

### Presentation 1 (10%)
**Week 11 | Group**

8-minute presentation on project progress and data governance findings.

**Week 13 | Group**

## Final Report & Poster (30%)
**Week 13 | Group**

Comprehensive group project report (~3,000 words) and poster for LegCo submission.

**Report Structure:**
1. Background
2. Identify specific decisions
3. Data available
4. Government data use
5. Mathematical modeling
6. Recommendations
